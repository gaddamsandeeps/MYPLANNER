To auto generate models/DAOs from database
--------------------------------------------------

sequelize-auto -h 'localhost' -d 'nisumplanner' -u 'root' -x 'root' -p '3307'  --dialect 'mysql' -o 'D:/nisum-planner/np/app/model'
